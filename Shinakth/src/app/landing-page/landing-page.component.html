Diabetic Retinopathy Image Segmentation

This web app uses a deep learning model to segment and analyze images of the retina to identify the presence and severity of Diabetic Retinopathy.

What is Diabetic Retinopathy?

Diabetic Retinopathy is a complication of diabetes that affects the eyes. It's caused by damage to the blood vessels of the light-sensitive tissue at the back of the eye (retina). At first, diabetic retinopathy may cause no symptoms or only mild vision problems. Eventually, it can cause blindness.
